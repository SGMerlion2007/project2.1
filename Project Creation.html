How Airbus Builds the Superjumbo Jet

Parts are manufactured in France, Germany, Spain, and the United Kingdom. As a result of the large size, conventional transport methods are not feasible. To combat this, the parts are sent to the Jean-Luc Lagardère Plant assembly hall in Toulouse, France, to be put together. Some of the parts are transported via specialized land and water transportation, whilst some parts are flown into Toulouse by the A300-600ST Beluga.
